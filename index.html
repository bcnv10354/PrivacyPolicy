VisionTranslate

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At VisionTranslate, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our keyboard extension app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

VisionTranslate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers beyond what's necessary for app functionality
  • User profiles or accounts

1.2 Keyboard Usage Data

Our keyboard extension may temporarily process:

  • Text you choose to translate (processed locally on your device)
  • Photos you select for text extraction (processed locally)
  • Language preferences for translation
Important: All text and image processing happens locally on your device. We do not send your typed text, photos, or translation content to external servers.

2. How We Use Information

The limited information processed by VisionTranslate is used solely for:

  • Providing translation services within the keyboard
  • Processing photos for text extraction (OCR)
  • Remembering your language preferences
  • Ensuring the app functions properly

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Processing: All translation and OCR processing occurs on your device using Apple's built-in frameworks
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your data on external servers or in the cloud
  • Subscription Data: Subscription status is managed through Apple's StoreKit framework and stored locally
  • App Group Sharing: Only subscription status is shared between the main app and keyboard extension using secure iOS App Groups

4. Third-Party Services

VisionTranslate uses the following Apple frameworks and services:

  • Translation Framework: For text translation (iOS 17.4+)
  • Vision Framework: For optical character recognition (OCR)
  • StoreKit: For subscription management
  • App Store Connect: For app distribution and analytics

These frameworks process data locally on your device and follow Apple's privacy standards.

5. Permissions

VisionTranslate may request the following permissions:

  • Full Access (Keyboard): Required for the keyboard to function and access translation features
  • Photo Library: Only when you choose to select photos for text extraction
  • Camera: Only when you choose to take photos for text extraction

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and no data is transmitted externally.

6. Children's Privacy

VisionTranslate is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since our app processes data locally and does not collect personal information, it complies with COPPA requirements.

7. International Users

VisionTranslate is designed to work globally and supports multiple languages. All data processing occurs locally on your device, regardless of your location, ensuring compliance with international privacy laws including GDPR.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users through the app if significant changes are made
  • Continue to respect your privacy in accordance with applicable laws

9. Your Rights

Since VisionTranslate does not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You maintain full control over:

  • The text you choose to translate
  • The photos you select for processing
  • Your subscription status through Apple's systems
  • Uninstalling the app at any time

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or VisionTranslate's privacy practices, please contact us:

  • App: VisionTranslate
  • Developer: Available through App Store Connect
  • Support: Through the App Store app page

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

This privacy policy is effective as of the last updated date above and applies to all users of VisionTranslate.
